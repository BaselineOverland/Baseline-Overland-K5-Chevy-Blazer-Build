Baseline Overland K5 Chevy Blazer Build

This is one of the Baseline Overland platform vehicles. It is built out as a daily driver outfitted for camping, biking, snowboarding, and reasonable offroad capability and drivability.

Specs:
White 1990 Chevy Blazer, with stock drivetrain with ~240K miles (350 small block, 700r4 transmission, NP241 transfer case, 10 bolt corporate axles).

  • Roof Rack – Baseline Overland
  • Diablo Front Bumper
  • Dual Swing Out Rear Bumper
  • Warn EVO12 S Winch + Factor 55 FlatLink E
  • Warn manual locking hubs replaced the facotry auto lockers.
  • Offroad Designs Custom Spring Lift – 2.5” lift.
  • Fox 2.0 Shocks
  • Power Stop Brakes
  • Optima Red Top Battery
  • Method MR701 Bead Grip wheels
  • 315/70R17 (35”) Milestar Patagonia M/T 02 Tires
  • Baja Designs Lighting (ONX6+ 20" Light Bar, XL Sport Amber, Squadron Sport Clear, Squadron SAE Amber)
  • Trigger Controller 6 shooter lighting controller
  • Nu-Relics Tailgat Regulator/ Motor
